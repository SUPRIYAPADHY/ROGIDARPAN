About Rogi Darpan

Rogi Darpan is a cutting-edge healthcare tracking system dedicated to revolutionizing patient management.   • Face Recognition Login: Seamless, secure login with advanced facial recognition technology.
  • Comprehensive Medical History: Instant access to detailed health records.
  • Emergency Contact Information: Quick access during urgent situations.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Navigate easily with an intuitive design.
  • Secure Data Management: Robust measures to protect health data confidentiality.
